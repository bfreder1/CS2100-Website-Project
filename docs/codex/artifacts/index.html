The Artifacts

The Artifacts are a collection of magical items and objects that can be found in the world. These artifacts are imbued with powerful magic, and are often sought after by heroes and villains alike. The Artifacts are divided into several categories, each dedicated to a different type of item.

The Armory
Name Type Summary
Spear of Athens Silver Lance Forged at the height of Athens' power, this spear channels the flames of war with the precision of a master tactician.
Calibur Holy Greatsword The story of this weapon is yet to be lived. It is prophesized that one day a great king of the north will wield this blade under a new name.
Robe of Jeff Robe An ancient relic form the Athenian monks, this robe was worn by the great cleric Jeff. It grants the wearer the protection of the Aegis itself.
Wonderous Items
Name Type Summary
Lyre of Apollo Instrument This golden lyre was gifted to the bard Orpheus by the god Apollo. It is said that the music played on this lyre can move the heavens themselves.
Ring of Khamnee Ring Gifted to patrons of the djini Khamnee, this ring's true nature is unknown. Legend says with 10 rings, one is granted dominion over the elemental planes.
